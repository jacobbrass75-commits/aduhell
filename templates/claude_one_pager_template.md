# One-Pager Template

Use this structure for a homeowner-facing ADU opportunity one-pager:

1. Headline opportunity statement
2. Property snapshot
3. What may fit here
4. Top ADU path to emphasize
5. Supporting alternative paths
6. Key caveats and diligence notes
7. Concept render caption with illustrative disclaimer
8. Next-step CTA

Rules:
- Use only approved facts from the property brief.
- Keep any concept render clearly labeled as illustrative only.
- Do not state that entitlement, cost, or feasibility is guaranteed.
